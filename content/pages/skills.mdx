# Skills

Skill modifier = modifier of the skill’s key ability score + proficiency bonus + other bonuses + penalties

Tests against skill Skill DC = 10 + Skill modifier

## Simple Difficulty Classes

| Task Difficulty    | Simple DC |
| :-------- | ---------: |
| Untrained |    10     |
| Trained   |    15     |
| Expert    |    20     |
| Master    |    30     |
| Legendary |    40     |

## Armor Penalty
Armor Penalty is applied to Strength and Dexterity based skill checks/DCs.

## Exploration/Downtime Activities
Exploration activities may take a minute or more. Downtime Activities may take a day or more. Usually can't be used during an encounter.
